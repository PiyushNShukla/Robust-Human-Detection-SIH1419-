Security and Surveillance:

Office Buildings: Monitor entry and exit points, ensuring only authorized personnel access restricted areas.

Retail Stores: Detect shoplifting, suspicious behavior, or unauthorized access to stockrooms.

Public Spaces: Enhance public safety in parks, transportation hubs, and crowded events by identifying unusual activities or security threats.

Perimeter Surveillance: The application integrates with surveillance cameras placed around the military base, providing real-time video feeds.

Human Detection and Tracking: Utilize computer vision models to detect and track human activity within and around the base. Assign unique identifiers to individuals for continuous tracking.

Geofencing: Define virtual geofences around sensitive areas within the base, such as weapon storage facilities, control rooms, and classified document repositories Define rules and thresholds for anomalies, such as unauthorized access, unusual movement patterns, or breaches of restricted areas.
